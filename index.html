About This Course

You don't need to be spiritual, flexible, or “good at clearing your mind”to meditate. In fact, most of what you've heard about meditation is over-complicated. This course strips away the jargon and gives you a practical, science-informed framework that fits into any lifestyle.

Each session is carefully designed to build on the last, so you never feel lost or overwhelmed. Whether you're dealing with stress, looking for better sleep, or simply curious — this is the gentlest on-ramp to a practice that can genuinely change the texture of your everyday life.

Built over two years of research and refined with feedback from over 2,000 students, The Inner Peace Journey is the meditation course we wish we'd had when we started.
